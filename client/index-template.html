Generic Ultimate Rainbow Race

Help

Generic Ultimate Rainbow Race is a multiplayer online game that can be played right here in your browser! So open a lobby, invite some friends and compete in this chaotic mix of jump 'n' run and level building!

Gameplay

After creating a lobby, selecting a level and hitting ‘Start Game’, you and your friends can get started! At the start of each turn, everyone can drag one block into the level to make it a little more insane. When the time runs out, the running begins - so try to get to the goal as fast as possible. The earlier you get there, the more points you get. You can also increase your score by collecting Kittens! The more turns you play, the crazier the level gets. Too hard? Try adding some platforms. Too easy? A machine gun will solve that!
